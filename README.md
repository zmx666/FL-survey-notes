## Classic Federated Learning Papers

### Foundational Works
- **Communication-Efficient Learning of Deep Networks from Decentralized Data**  
  McMahan et al., AISTATS 2017  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1602.05629

- **Federated Learning: Strategies for Improving Communication Efficiency**  
  McMahan et al., NIPS Workshop 2016  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1610.05492

- **Federated Optimization in Heterogeneous Networks**  
  Kairouz et al., MLSys 2021  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2009.06303

### Client Heterogeneity & Personalization
- **Federated Optimization in Heterogeneous Networks**  
  Li et al. (FedProx), MLSys 2020  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.06127

- **Model-Agnostic Meta-Learning for Fast Adaptation of Deep Networks**  
  Finn et al., ICML 2017  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1703.03400

- **Personalized Federated Learning with Moreau Envelopes**  
  Dinh et al., NeurIPS 2020  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.08848

### System & Optimization-Oriented FL
- **SCAFFOLD: Stochastic Controlled Averaging for Federated Learning**  
  Karimireddy et al., ICML 2020  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1910.06378

- **FedNova: Normalizing Local Updates in Federated Learning**  
  Wang et al., NeurIPS 2020  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/2007.07481

### Privacy & Security
- **Practical Secure Aggregation for Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning**  
  Bonawitz et al., CCS 2017  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.04482

- **Deep Learning with Differential Privacy**  
  Abadi et al., CCS 2016  
  https://arxiv.org/abs/1607.00133
